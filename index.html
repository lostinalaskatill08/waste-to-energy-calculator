Waste-to-Energy Calculator

Assumptions Used in the Calculator:

  • Each person generates:
    • 435 kg of food waste per year.
    • 500 kg of plastic/MSW (municipal solid waste) per year.
    • 50 kg of human waste (sewage biosolids) per year.
  • Food waste is converted into **biogas** through anaerobic digestion.
  • Plastic and MSW are **incinerated** like the H-Power plant in Hawaii.
  • Human waste is also **processed via anaerobic digestion**.
  • Conversion rates:
    • **100 m³ of methane per ton** of food/human waste.
    • **10 kWh of electricity per m³ of methane**.
    • **0.42 MWh of electricity per ton** of incinerated plastic/MSW.
    • **1.5 cubic meters of landfill space saved per ton** processed.
    • **2.5 tons of CO₂ saved per ton of food/human waste diverted**.
    • **0.2 tons of algae per ton of CO₂ captured**.
    • **0.5 tons of biofuel per ton of algae produced**.
  • Average household electricity usage is **5000 kWh per year**.
